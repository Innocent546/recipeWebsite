Crispy chicken strips

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500gms
  • Flour: 100gms
  • Seasoning: 20gms
  • Baking Powder: 50gms

Instructions

  1. Marinade the chicken tenders. 30 minutes at room temperature is ideal. Don’t put it in the fridge or the breading will fall off.
  2. Coat the fingers in the flour mix. Don’t pat them dry before doing this. They need to have the marinade on them to help the flour stick.
  3. Dip the chicken fingers into the whisked eggs.
  4. Coat the fingers a second time. Don’t pat flat, really let it get thick on the chicken. You need all the nooks and crannies to get the desired crispy consistency.
  5. Fry in the oil (or bake them) until ready. Drain on a wire rack (NOT paper towel!) and serve while they are still warm. Enjoy!
