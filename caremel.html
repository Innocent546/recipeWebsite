Caremel pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

HOW TO MAKE ?

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the sugar until it becomes a deep amber color. Be patient and resist the urge to stir, as this will make the sugar crystallize.
  2. Carefully remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk.
  5. Pour the custard into the prepared ramekins or baking dish.
  6. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  7. Cover the puddings with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or up to overnight.
